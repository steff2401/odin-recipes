Wok

Description

A quick to make wok recipe with lamb meat, vegetables and noodles. For 1 person.

Ingredients

  • 150 g lamb chops
  • 75 g noodles
  • 1/4 red onion
  • 1/4 red bell pepper
  • 75 g leaf cabbage
  • 1/2 carrot
  • 1 tablespoon rapeseed oil
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1/4 glass pre-made wok sauce

Steps

  1. Let the meat thaw on the kitchen counter.
  2. Cook the noodles following the package's instructions. When done cooking, pour out the water and rinse in cold water.
  3. Cut red onion into boats, bell pepper into pieces, leaf cabbage into strips and carrot into stick
  4. Fry the meat quickly in oil in several batches in a really hot pan. Season with ginger.
  5. Add vegetables after 3-4 minutes, and cook for another 3-4 minutes until everything is cooked.
  6. Add noodles, wok sauce and possibly a little water.
