Wok

Description

A quick to make wok recipe with lamb meat, vegetables and noodles.

Ingredients

  • 150 g lamb chops
  • 75 g noodles
  • 1/4 red onion
  • 1/4 red bell pepper
  • 75 g leaf cabbage
  • 1/2 carrot
  • 1 tablespoon rapeseed oil
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1/4 glass pre-made wok sauce
