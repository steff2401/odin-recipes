Waffles

Description

Delicious waffles, a guaranteed success for all ages!

Ingredients

  • 4 dL wheat flour
  • 1 dL sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoons ground cardamom
  • 4 dL milk
  • 3 eggs
  • 100 g melted butter

Steps

  1. Put all the dry ingredients in a bowl and whisk in a little milk at a time. Stir well between each time to get a smooth batter without milk lumps.
  2. Stir in the eggs and add the melted butter. Let the batter swell for 1/2 hour. Adjust the batter with a little water or milk if it is too thick.
  3. Fry the waffles and serve them warm. Usually served with jam, sour cream, sugar and/or butter.
