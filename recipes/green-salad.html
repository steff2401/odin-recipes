Green salad

Description

A healthy and very easy to make green salad consisting of endive, arugula and red onion

Ingredients

  • 2 endives
  • 35 g arugula
  • 1 red onion
  • 1/2 lemon

Steps

  1. Rinse the lettuce/endives and arugula well.
  2. Break it into smaller pieces with your hands and put it in a large salad bowl or on a plate.
  3. Cut the red onion into boats as thin as possible and place them in a bowl.
  4. Squeeze over some lemon juice and mix well.
  5. Leave for a few minutes to let the lemon juice make the onion more mild. Place the onion onto the salad right before serving.
  6. Often served with balsamic vinegar, olive oil, salt and/or pepper.
